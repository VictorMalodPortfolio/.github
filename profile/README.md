# Soft skills

- I am a **very** collaborative teammate, I work with people every day with passion and smile on my face, _especially when we are fixing the burning production_. I am very communicative human being (verbal and written), if there is a tension, I'm going to talk to you about it, even if you would rather avoid it :feelsgood:.
- Working with other people is **essential** for me. Sitting alone in a corner of the room would just make me die in place. Developping software alone is **slow**, **lacks** of knowledge **sharing**, **not challenging**, and **sad**...
- New tech ? No problem. Give me _`<timescale according to complexity of the subject goes here>`_ of experiment and I will have strong basics. I've done this many times before.
- I am a proactive engineer, I am going to push my concerns and ideas to the team and managers and will assume any responsability you want me to take. I led teams accross the world (mostly Ukraine, Portugal, and the United-States), and we always managed to deliver applications that created trust and credibility :fire:.
- **I am not** an **over** engineer. I know time is a **very valuable** resource, and I will always evaluate the tradeoffs and think before building or validating any part of a software. I am never going to spend multiple days on insignificant task, just for the fun of it. I am not a cat in the field that will intterupt its journey because it saw a butterfly flying by.
- I pay extra attention to detail, and you will wonder how you could not spot this before the PR review you sent me. 
- I love taking inputs from others, I dislike building something alone: I know I could be missing something obvious to someone else. (Now we have AI, sure, but AI is so bad when it doesn't have the whole context of a project :rage4:)
- I love solving problems. That's what engineering is about. Repetitive tasks with no creativity aren't my thing. I naturally lean toward the hardest problems first.
